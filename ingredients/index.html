Our Ingredients

Every ingredient is chosen for its effectiveness, safety, and sustainability.

Hyaluronic Acid

A naturally occurring molecule that holds up to 1,000 times its weight in water. Our multi-weight formula penetrates multiple skin layers for deep, lasting hydration that plumps and smooths from within.

Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid)

A potent antioxidant that brightens skin, fades hyperpigmentation, and stimulates collagen production. Our stabilized formula maintains potency longer than conventional vitamin C serums.

Rosehip Oil

Cold-pressed from Rosa canina seeds, rich in vitamins A and C and essential fatty acids. Repairs damaged skin, reduces scarring, and provides deep nourishment without clogging pores.

Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)

Strengthens the skin barrier, reduces pore appearance, and regulates oil production. Also effective at fading dark spots and reducing redness, making it ideal for acne-prone and sensitive skin.

Squalane

Plant-derived from olives, this lightweight oil mimics your skin's natural sebum. Provides non-greasy hydration, locks in moisture, and creates a smooth, dewy finish suitable for all skin types.
