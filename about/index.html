Our Story

Born from a passion for clean beauty and a commitment to your skin's health.

The Lohm Philosophy

Lohm Skincare was founded on a simple belief: your skincare should be as pure as nature intended. We saw an industry filled with synthetic chemicals, misleading labels, and products that prioritized marketing over actual results. We knew there had to be a better way — skincare that's honest, effective, and kind to both your skin and the planet.

Every product in our line begins with research into the most effective natural ingredients available. We work with botanists, dermatologists, and cosmetic chemists to develop formulas that harness the power of plant-based actives without compromising on performance. The result is skincare that feels luxurious, delivers visible results, and aligns with your values.

Our Commitment

Sustainability isn't a marketing buzzword for us — it's woven into every decision we make. From sourcing ingredients from certified organic farms to using post-consumer recycled packaging, we're constantly looking for ways to reduce our environmental footprint. We partner with fair-trade cooperatives to ensure the communities that grow our ingredients benefit from our success.

We're also committed to radical transparency. Every product page includes a complete ingredient breakdown explaining what each component does and why we chose it. No proprietary blends, no hidden fillers, no fine print. If we wouldn't put it on our own skin, we won't put it on yours.

For Every Skin Type

We believe beautiful skin isn't one-size-fits-all. Our products are formulated to work across all skin types — dry, oily, combination, sensitive, and mature. We develop and test our formulas on diverse skin tones and conditions to ensure they deliver consistent results for everyone. Because everyone deserves skincare that actually works for them.
